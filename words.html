Financial Terms

  • Asset: An economic benefit that is controlled by a person or organization.
  • Book value: The original cost of an asset minus any depreciation.
  • Capital: Assets that can be used to produce goods or services.
  • Credit: An agreement to pay for a good or service at a later date.
  • Credit rating: A ranking of a person or business's ability to repay debt.
  • Current asset: An asset that can be converted to cash within one year.
  • Current liability: A liability that is due within one year.
  • Liability: A legal obligation to repay a debt.
